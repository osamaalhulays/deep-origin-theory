# Final Editorial Decision

## Official title

`Deep Origin Theory: A Source-Governed Framework for Curvature Response and Cosmological Dynamics`

## Primary category

`gr-qc`

## Claim posture

Deep Origin Theory is presented here as:

- a source-governed theoretical framework
- a locked physical kernel
- a set of auditable response surfaces

Deep Origin Theory is not presented here as:

- a completed theory of everything
- a final observational cosmology
- a learned emulator
- a post-hoc fitting patch
- a completed unresolved-tail closure

## Governing Bridge Law placement

`The Governing Bridge Law` remains part of the internal identity of the theory.

It may appear as:

- an internal formulation name
- a section title
- or a principle inside the manuscript

It should not appear as the main outward-facing deposit title.

## Main deposit contents

The main paper should contain:

- Deep Origin Theory as one theory
- the source-governed response principle
- the locked physical kernel
- the mathematical core
- the curvature-response / cosmological-dynamics relation
- falsifiability and reproducibility posture
- scope limits and non-claims
