This note exists so the reviewer can distinguish between:

- what is already being claimed,
- what is deliberately deferred,
- and what remains tracked as a live debt.

## Why this matters

A reviewer should not have to spend time enumerating rows we already track
openly ourselves.

The right review question is not:

- “have they admitted nothing remains?”

The right question is:

- “given what they openly defer, are the current claims still honest,
  meaningful, and worth advancing?”
